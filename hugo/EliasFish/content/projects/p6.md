# Introduction
Catstronaur: Mission Impawssible is a 3D platformer made as the 6th game project at The Game Assembly. The platforming and movement is based on Mario 64 while some things like the camera are based on Mario Oddyssey for a more modern feel. The goal is to visit different planets to pick up your lost fish oil cargo in hopes of getting employee of the month!

### Landscape Tessellation
With many of our levels making use of landscape, optimization became a clear issue since due to the landscape being a huge plane it couldn't be frustum culled like other meshes. To avoid rendering millions of vertices all the time me and [another programmer](https://www.albin.foo) started looking into tessellation. With a tessellation pipeline we could generate the entire landscape at runtime with a combination of a hull, domain and geometry shader making it possible to skip generating certain parts of the mesh that weren't visible in the scene.
